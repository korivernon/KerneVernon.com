About

Precision Home Solutions is a locally owned pressure washing and handyman contractor business operating out of Orange, NJ that serves Essex, Union, Passaic & Morris Counties. We offer a variety of residential and commercial pressure washing services and take the stress out of doing quality handyman jobs such as furniture and equipment assembly, small electrical repairs/installation, and other odd jobs. We are dedicated to delivering quality, professional customer care for an exceptional fair and friendly customer experience. Contact us today for your free estimate on your next project or if you have any questions and need more information concerning our provided services. We are looking forward to working with you in the near future!!!

Business Services

Pressure Washing Services

Precision Home Solutions offers affordable pressure washing siding cleaning services to residential homes and commercial businesses in the surrounding counties listed above. Siding maintenance is extremely important in the harsh local climate and over time, algae and dirt can begin to degrade exterior surface materials. The professional cleaning services are provided by using safe yet effective products which reach deep into the surface to safely break down and rinse away mold, mildew, and other damaging material. Not only are you protecting the service life of your residence, but also maintaining and promoting a clean property.

We also power wash concrete driveways, sidewalks, walkways, patios, retaining walls, decks and do fence restoration.
